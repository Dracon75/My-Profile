About


From the United States Midwest and raised in the state of Michigan, I have been interested in technology and natural resource conservation for as long as I can remember. Living next to the largest source of surface fresh water on the planet along with a natural love for the outdoors have fueled my desire to be a good steward, protecting Earths resources. I love to travel, explore and learn. Have you ever noticed that environmentalists and naturalists tend not to be very fast at keeping up with the world of tech? Because of this I am currently learning to become a full stack web developer and hope to incorporate what I learn to help bridge the gap between the environment and the world of technology.
